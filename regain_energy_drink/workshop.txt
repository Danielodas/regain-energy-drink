description=[h2]What is this mod?[/h2]

description=

description=This mod adds Regain, a discontinued Japanese energy drink that was first aimed at businessmen. Now it has made its way to Kentucky. It decreases your fatigue, but it can also reduce pain and food sickness. It really is an energy drink that can act as medicine. You might be thinking it's overpowered, [b]it's not[/b], despite its characteristics, it has an [b]extremely low[/b] spawn rate. Will you collect them or drink them?
